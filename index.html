Yogi Hemant Upadhyay is the founder of Sarvarogahara Wellness Center. Yogi Hemant Upadhyay was born to a very religious and pious family in Varanasi, India. He organized one of the largest meditation programs of over 8000+ people during the pandemic year of 2020 for a non-governmental organization.

He believes that healing is deeply influenced by the mind, the relationships one fosters in family and one‚Äôs overall approach towards life. Hence, over and above the healing techniques, he also imparts counselling sessions. Yogi Hemant is a certified counsellor and a life coach and offers innate relationship and family counselling to his clients.

At Sarvarogahara, Yogi Hemant aims to spread the knowledge of peace and love, far and wide through his existence. He has been a motivational speaker at several seminars and workshops. One of his prime moto, is to spread happiness across the world by uplifting the state of consciousness of people.
